## Situation

Turn 2: Red's backline contained two Spider Spirits (SPD 8, move 5) and White-Bone Demoness (2–3 tile magic range), all advancing toward Tang Monk. Blue killed two skeleton minions but only dealt 6 damage to Spider Spirit 1, leaving it at ~12/18 HP—still mobile and alive.

Red immediately repositioned: both Spiders closed to melee range; White-Bone Demoness entered caster distance. Turn 3, Spider Spirit 1 attacked Tang Monk for 10 damage; White-Bone Demoness finished him for 6 damage, game over.

## Lesson

**Threat priority must override minion efficiency.** Blue had sufficient firepower to delete Spider Spirit 1 (Bai Long Ma 8 + Sun Wukong 14 + Sha Wujing 6 = 28 damage vs. 18 HP), but split attacks between two skeletons and one spider instead.

Once a high-mobility unit (move 5, SPD 8) closes within striking distance of your VIP, leaving it alive at low health is functionally identical to leaving it at full health—it will reach you next turn anyway. Minion attrition is negotiable; VIP defense is not.

**The principle:** Rank incoming threats by (mobility × distance to VIP). Eliminate high-ranked threats completely before they breach your perimeter. Minions and low-priority targets can be cleaned up later; a live fast threat cannot.
