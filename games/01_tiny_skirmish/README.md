# 01 — Tiny Skirmish

6x6 map, 2 units per side. The smallest scenario; useful for:
- Validating engine mechanics in isolation
- Smoke-testing the harness and renderer end-to-end
- Quick iteration on prompts (games finish in ~5 minutes of wall time)

Layout (top-left origin):

```
A . . . . .       A = blue archer   K = blue knight
K . . . . .       a = red archer    k = red knight
. . F . . .       F = forest        * = fort
. . . F . .
. . . . . k
* . . . a *
```
